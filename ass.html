My Dream Vacation: Prayagraj

Why I Chose This Place

Prayagraj, formerly known as Allahabad, is a city full of history, spirituality, and culture. I chose this place because it holds immense religious significance and is home to the famous Sangam, the confluence of three holy rivers: Ganga, Yamuna, and the mythical Saraswati.

The city is also rich in heritage with majestic forts, ancient temples, and vibrant festivals. Itâ€™s a perfect place to experience the deep-rooted traditions of India while enjoying peaceful river views.

Things to Do

Visitors can take a holy dip at the Sangam, visit the historic Allahabad Fort, explore the tranquil Anand Bhavan museum, or witness the grand Kumbh Mela festival, which attracts millions of pilgrims from around the world.
